Born and raised...

Is it in poor taste to use an "About Me" page to self promote my adoration for my hometown? Yup! Born in Las Vegas, Nevada, and I'll always love it there! The round-the-clock hustle and bustle of the city that never sleeps. Growing up in the beatiful Green Valley suburbs, I'd like to think of my childhood as a great one, and the diverse culture and the constant happenings around town definitely shaped me up to be a rather fine gentleman, if I may say so myself!

Any...relatable skills perhaps?

I've always been drawn to mediums that let my imagination flourish and bring those nifty ideas to life. At an early age, I absolutely loved to play the piano. It was such a beautiful thing to play, and an even more beautiful thing to hear. As my passion and abilities strengthened, I looked to create my own renditions of mesmerizing musical pieces that I wanted to bring to life with my own hands, and mesmerizing they were! As I grew older, I discovered the magical world of Photoshop. To have my ideas and passions be visually appreciated was nothing short of a dream and was the best thing I had ever poured my soul into...

Until I discovered web programming. This is the big catch! If I ever really wanted to allow others (and myself) to dynamically interact and love my creations, this was definitely the place to be. I'm still a beginner, and what I know of web development mostly lies in Ruby. I hit several Ruby learning tools hard (I credit primarily Treehouse, Chris Pine's "Learn to Program," and GA's "Dash" for the knowledge I've been able to soak up) and while this learning experience has definitely been the most taxing, as well as rewarding, I find pride in what I am able to create and understand. Recently, I have turned to General Assembly's WDI program in anticipation of the great opportunity to work and learn with others who share this passion that I do for the art of programming.
